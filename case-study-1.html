Final Four Music Video

Media Type: Video

This project was my first foray into video editing. It was created for an art class called "Intro to Electronic Media", and it was a solo project meant to test our knowledge of Adobe Premier Pro in class. Students were given the freedom to choose whatever song and footage they wanted for the video, but they had to include certain video effects and display the lyrics onscreen every few times in the video.
